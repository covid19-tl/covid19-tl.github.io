Testing

As of April 27, 2020, Timor-Leste has made 480 tests. That’s more tests per 1,000 people than Indonesia, but less that the Philippines or Vietnam.

From the 480 tests conducted:

  • 24 came back positive
  • 367 came back negative
  • 89 are pending
