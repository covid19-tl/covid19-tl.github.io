Total and daily confirmed COVID-19 cases

Timor-Leste has 24 confirmed cases, 22 of which are in Dili, and 2 in Liquica.

How does Timor compare?

Timor has less confirmed cases per million than Indonesia or the Philippines

Testing

As of May 1, 2020, Timor-Leste has made 671 tests. That’s more tests per 1,000 people than Indonesia, but less that the Philippines or Vietnam.

From the 671 tests conducted:

  • 24 came back positive
  • 478 came back negative
  • 169 are pending
